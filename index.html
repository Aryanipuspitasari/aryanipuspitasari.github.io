About

Hi there !

Welcome to my website! I'm thrilled to have opportunity to share my story with you. My name is Ary and come from Indonesia but currently base in Germany. In the following sections, you'll get a glimpse into my educational background, work experiences, and aspirations. I hope that my journey resonates with you and sparks a sense of inspiration. I encourage you to explore further and discover the various facets of my life and career.

Education Background

I obtained my Bachelor of Japanese Literature from Surabaya State University, Indonesia. I know that education background and what I'm currently learning are not related, but This brief overview of my educational background demonstrates my commitment to personal growth and lays the groundwork for the experiences and expertise I have acquired along the way.

Work Experiences

I had the privilege to work in the hospitality sector for 4 and 5 star hotels in Indonesia as a front desk agent as well as working in research and development specialist shoe footwear for five years with two Japanese companies and several European companies. This experience has enriched my expertise in both fields.

I look forward to having new experiences

My passion for learning and growth continues to drive me as I embark on new ventures. With an optimistic mindset and a willingness to embrace new challenges, I am committed to ongoing personal and career development. I am excited to embark on this journey of growth, cultivating meaningful connections, and making a positive impact on both local and global scales. With a solid foundation in front-end development, I look forward to collaborating on projects that challenge me to push boundaries, create engaging user interfaces, and deliver seamless user experiences. I aspire to leverage my expertise in website development to contribute to the success of businesses and organizations by building intuitive and visually appealing websites.
